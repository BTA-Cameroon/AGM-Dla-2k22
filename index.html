About Us

Ex-Students of Presbyterian Secondary School (P.S.S) Mankon

PEMEXA is an association of ex-students of Presbyterian Secondary School (P.S.S.) Mankon and PEMEXA Cameroon is the governing head office.

Presbyterian Secondary School Mankon (aka P.S.S. Mankon) is one of the most prestigious secondary and high school institutions in Cameroon. It is located in Bamenda, in the North West Region of Cameroon, and is one of the great landmarks of the Region, and in the nation. P.S.S. Mankon consistently records outstanding results in the Cameroon General Certificate of Education (GCE) Examinations and is the alma mater of some of the greatest minds that Cameroon can boast.
