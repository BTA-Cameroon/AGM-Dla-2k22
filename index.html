About Us

PEMEXA Douala branch has10 Years Of Experience

PEMEXA is the ex-students of Presbyterian Secondary School (P.S.S.) Mankon and pemexa Cameroon in particular is the governor head office of all the worldwide branches. The Presbyterian Secondary School Mankon (aka P.S.S. Mankon) is one of the most prestigious secondary and high school institutions in Cameroon. It is located in Bamenda, in the North West Region of Cameroon, and is one of the great landmarks of the province, and of the nation.

P.S.S. Mankon consistently records outstanding results in the Cameroon General Certificate of Education (GCE) Examinations, and is the alma mater of some of the greatest minds that Cameroon can boast of.
